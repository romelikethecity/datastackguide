Best Resources for Data Stack Professionals in 2026

The modern data stack moves fast. Tools emerge, get acquired, pivot, or die every quarter. Staying current requires the right mix of newsletters, communities, and practitioner blogs that cut through vendor marketing.

This list is built for data engineers, analytics engineers, and anyone who builds and maintains data infrastructure. Every resource here was recommended by practitioners, not sponsors.

Newsletters

1. SeattleDataGuy's Newsletter

109K+ subscribers. Covers end-to-end data flows, data engineering, MLOps, and data science.

2. Data Engineering Weekly

Weekly curated newsletter by Ananth Packkildurai covering data infrastructure and engineering news.

3. DataExpert.io Newsletter

Zach Wilson's newsletter on data engineering and tech trends. 113K+ subscribers.

4. Data Elixir

Weekly curated newsletter of top data science content covering ML, visualization, analytics, and strategy.

Blogs & Websites

1. dbt Blog

Official blog covering analytics engineering best practices, dbt features, and community highlights.

2. Locally Optimistic

Community blog for current and aspiring data analytics leaders sharing real-world experience.

3. Data Stack Guide OUR PICK

Independent data stack tool reviews, architecture guides, and comparison resources.

Communities

1. dbt Community

100K+ member community for analytics engineers. Slack (66K+), forums, and annual dbt Summit conference.

2. DataTalks.Club

Free Data Engineering Zoomcamp course, active Slack community, and regular data engineering meetups.

3. Data Engineer Things

Global community built by data engineers offering articles, webinars, meetups, and mentorship.

4. Data Council

Conference and community for data engineers, data scientists, and analytics professionals.

Tools Worth Knowing

1. Kaggle

23M+ accounts. Competitions, datasets, notebooks, and community discussions for data practitioners.

Podcasts

1. Data Engineering Podcast

Tobias Macey unpacks data infrastructure, analytics, and modern data management techniques.

2. DataFramed

Weekly podcast exploring how AI and data are changing the world, featuring data and AI leaders.

Courses & Training

1. DataCamp

Interactive data engineering and data science courses covering Python, SQL, dbt, Spark, and more.

How We Curated This List

Three criteria. First, does this resource teach you something you can't learn from a Google search? Second, is it actively maintained and producing new content? Third, do practitioners in the role actually recommend it to peers? We don't accept payment for listings. We review and update this page quarterly.
